The Proxy Program: An Invitation

You've just experienced the power of scent to unlock memory, emotion, and deep states of consciousness. Now, we invite you to help share it.

Become a Local Guide for a Global Journey

The Proxy Program is a partnership for hypnotists and wellness practitioners like you. It’s an opportunity to bring The Incense Journey™, a live, virtual event led by John Launius, to your local community.

As a Proxy, you are not the performer; you are the anchor. You hold the space, prepare the sacred scents, and facilitate a seamless, profound experience for your guests while they connect to the live-streamed event. You become the bridge between the ancient art of incense and modern hypnosis.

You provide the space. John provides the guidance. The incense provides the transformation.

Why You'll Love Being a Proxy

Expand Your Practice

Add a unique, powerful, and recurring event to your calendar (every 6-8 weeks). Offer your existing clients something new and attract a wider audience interested in deep, sensory experiences. This is your chance to showcase your own local offerings to an engaged group.

Harness the Limbic System

As a hypnotist, you know the power of the subconscious. Scent offers a direct, non-verbal pathway to the limbic system, the seat of memory and emotion. By hosting, you facilitate profound, lasting change for your participants in a way that words alone cannot.

Join a Community

You won't be doing this alone. You'll become part of a passionate, international community of practitioners dedicated to exploring consciousness. Share insights, get support, and grow together with fellow Proxies and John.

Create a New Revenue Stream

This partnership includes a generous revenue-sharing model. It's a simple way to supplement your income by doing what you love: creating transformative experiences for others.

What's Involved? The Simple Logistics

Your Role

  • Secure a quiet, comfortable space for your group.
  • Promote the event to your community.
  • Handle local registrations and payments.
  • Set up a simple viewing station (TV/monitor & speakers).
  • Prepare the provided incense materials before the event.

Our Commitment to You

  • A unique Zoom link for each event.
  • All incense materials and descriptions sent to you.
  • Promotional materials to help you market the event.
  • Clear instructions and a pre-event check-in with John.
  • A straightforward revenue-sharing agreement.

The goal is a turnkey experience. We provide the content and materials so you can focus on your community.
