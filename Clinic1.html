A comprehensive analysis of thermal sweet spots, active molecules, and safety profiles for resin-based clinical interventions.

Boswellia sacra

Frankincense
Black Grade

Grounding (Non-Sedating)
Thermal Sweet Spot
160–190 °C
Key Active Molecules
Incensole Acetate (Diterpene), Incensole, α-pinene, limonene (early phase).
Mechanism

TRPV3 ion channel agonism (central anxiolytic). Limbic modulation & NF-κB inhibition.

Safety
Overheating >220°C produces burnt sugar/acrid odor.
Santalum album

Sandalwood

Deep Sedative
Thermal Sweet Spot
190–220 °C
Key Active Molecules
α-Santalol, β-Santalol (sesquiterpenols).
Mechanism

Central nervous system sedation. Parasympathetic activation. Reduction of physiological arousal.

Safety
Wood chars quickly >230°C. Precise control required.
Commiphora myrrha

Black Myrrh

Analgesic / Grounding
Thermal Sweet Spot
130–170 °C
Key Active Molecules
Furanosesquiterpenes, Furanoeudesma-1,3-diene, Curzerene.
Mechanism

Opioid receptor interaction (analgesic). COX/LOX inhibition (anti-inflammatory).

Safety
200°C → "burnt rubber" odor. Polarizing scent.
Dryobalanops aromatica

Borneol Camphor

Rapid Acute Calm
Thermal Sweet Spot
150–180 °C
Key Active Molecules
(+)-Borneol, Isoborneol (minor).
Mechanism

GABA-A modulation, BBB permeability enhancement, TRPM8 (cooling) activation.

Safety
Avoid synthetic camphor. High doses → CNS toxicity risk.
Pistacia khinjuk

Khinjuk Mastic

Clarifying
Thermal Sweet Spot
80–120 °C
Key Active Molecules
Phellandrene, α-Pinene, trace limonene.
Mechanism

Cholinergic modulation (AChE inhibition), Bronchodilation.

Safety
Oxidized pinene may irritate airways. Not sedating.
Cistus ladanifer

Labdanum

Deep Grounding
Thermal Sweet Spot
110–150 °C
Key Active Molecules
Labdane diterpenes, Ledol, Viridiflorol.
Mechanism

Likely GABAergic / calcium channel modulation. Airborne antimicrobial activity.

Safety
Extremely sticky. Water content may sputter.
Liquidambar orientalis

Black Storax

Clarifying / Neutral
Thermal Sweet Spot
100–140 °C
Key Active Molecules
Styrene, Cinnamic acid derivatives, Vanillin.
Mechanism

Cholinergic modulation. Antimicrobial vapor phase activity.

Safety
Styrene accumulation → ventilate. Avoid prolonged saturation.
Styrax tonkinensis

Benzoin Siam

Comforting
Thermal Sweet Spot
90–130 °C
Key Active Molecules
Coniferyl benzoate, Benzoic acid, Vanillin.
Mechanism

Respiratory mucosal soothing. Positive associative memory anchoring.

Safety
Overheating degrades esters → acridity.
Bursera / Protium spp.

Red Copal

Uplifting Grounding
Thermal Sweet Spot
140–180 °C
Key Active Molecules
Limonene, Germacrene, β-Caryophyllene, Amyrins (non-volatile).
Mechanism

GABA-benzodiazepine receptor modulation. CB2 receptor interaction.

Safety
200°C → acrid smoke onset.
Dracaena cochinchinensis

Dragon's Blood

Visual / Contextual
Thermal Sweet Spot
180–220 °C
Key Active Molecules
Flavonoids (Loureirins A–C), Trace β-caryophyllene.
Mechanism

Minimal via inhalation. Trace CB2 interaction. Primarily used for visual/sensory context.

Safety
High residue. Easily chars >250 °C.
