What Is a Proxy?

A Proxy hosts a local group of attendees during The Incense Journey™, which is led live via Zoom by Komoto John Launius. Your role is to create a grounded, attentive environment, prepare the incense materials in advance, and guide your guests through the journey alongside the Komoto’s presentation.

You are not performing or leading. You are holding space—with presence, care, and readiness.

Core Principles

Purpose

The Incense Journey™ is a technology for consciousness transformation, offering direct, embodied experiences through the subtle act of listening to fragrance. Each incense opens a doorway into memory, mood, and awareness.

“The journey begins not with burning incense, but with listening.”

The Role of the Komoto

John Launius, as Komoto, guides participants in discovering the Way of Incense by:

  • Presenting each incense offering with context and insight
  • Prompting reflections and questions
  • Welcoming all backgrounds and experience levels
  • Leading without imposing, letting the incense do the work

As a proxy, your job is to support the Komoto by creating a seamless, prepared local experience.

Equipment & Technical Setup

Required Equipment

  • ◆Electric incense warmer (Golden Lotus recommended). Use setting 260 (~325°F surface temp).
  • ◆Warmer Timer: Ensure the warmer's timer is set for a minimum of 2.5 hours to cover the 30-minute pre-heat and the 2-hour session.
  • ◆8 foil incense cups, pre-filled
  • ◆Teaspoon for measuring resin
  • ◆Tweezers and spoon for materials
  • ◆Hand fan (Japanese, bamboo, or feather)
  • ◆TV or monitor connected to a computer
  • ◆External speakers or soundbar
  • ◆Reliable internet connection

Optional:

  • External microphone

Technical Readiness

  • ◆Laptop/computer plugged in, fully charged
  • ◆Phone fully charged and nearby as backup
  • ◆Zoom link, dial-in, and ID ready

Resin Prep & Handling

Use 1 level teaspoon per incense offering (unless otherwise noted). Pre-fill all 8 foil cups and have them ready in order before the session begins.

Material Action
Sticky resinsFreeze 24–48 hrs before grinding
KyphiCut a small piece (about the size of a pea) with a knife; do not grind.
Borneol CamphorUse as-is
Wood chipsUse as-is

Clean tools with high-proof alcohol. Recommended source for natural, untreated incense: YENology (discount codes often available).

During the Journey

The journey is 2 hours long with 8 incense offerings (~15 minutes each).

Sequence:

  1. Preheat your warmer for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Start the first incense cup as the session begins.
  3. Follow the Komoto’s cues for when to swap incense.
  4. Use your fan to gently circulate the aroma.
  5. Seat guests close to the warmer for optimal experience.
  6. Maintain a quiet, respectful, and attentive space.

Post-Journey & Administration

Reflection & Engagement

Support guests by using gentle eye contact and open posture. Relay questions to the Komoto via Zoom chat or by unmuting at an appropriate time.

Administrative Tasks

  • Collect payment from attendees before the session.
  • Refer to your signed agreement for revenue share, payment terms, and deadlines.
  • After the session, provide John with the full names and email addresses of all attendees.
  • Distribute the list of incense descriptions and notes at the end of the session for guests to take home.
