Gentle Warming of Resins

Welcome to the beautiful practice of heating resins. Unlike burning on charcoal, gentle warming releases the pure, complex aromatics of the resin without the smoke of combustion. Below is your starter kit.

1. The Warming Setup

The core of this practice relies on applying gentle, controlled heat to your aromatics. These tools ensure a clean, precise heating environment.

The Heart of the Practice

3. The Pure Resins

These are your foundational aromatics. Gently warming these high-quality pure resins will reveal layers of scent you've never experienced before.

Labdanum

Sweet, Ambery & Deep

A thick, sticky, tar-like resin. Do not grind this! Put it in the freezer for 24 hours before using, then break off a pea-sized piece and place it directly in your dish to warm.

Purchase Labdanum

Boswellia Sacra

Royal Hojary Frankincense

The highest grade of Frankincense. Freeze for 24 hours before grinding to make it less sticky, and store in glass jars to protect the scent. Gently warmed, it releases uplifting citrus and pine notes.

Purchase B. Sacra

Benzoin Sumatra

Warm Vanilla Notes

A deeply comforting resin with distinct notes of vanilla. Like Frankincense, freeze for 24 hours before grinding to reduce stickiness and store in a jar to protect its beautiful aromatic profile.

Purchase Benzoin
