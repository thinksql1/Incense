Primary Procurement Strategy

The most efficient approach is to consolidate the majority of your order from Apothecary's Garden. They are a premier Canadian supplier based in Hamilton, ON, known for exceptional quality, ethical sourcing, and deep botanical expertise. Placing a single order over $100 USD with them will also qualify for free shipping within Canada.

This guide details the specific products to purchase from them, along with vetted alternatives for the few items they do not carry.
