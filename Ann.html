Resin Preparation & Handling

Resin Dishes (Incense Cone Holders)

These bottle-cap sized holders are perfect for resins. Line them with a small piece of food-grade aluminum foil for easy cleanup and no waste.

View on Amazon

Coffee Grinder for Resins

An electric coffee grinder works well for breaking down resins. Pro Tip: Freeze the resin for 48 hours before grinding to reduce stickiness.

View on Amazon

Foil Sheets & Cleaning

Consider pre-cutting these foil sheets, bagging them, and selling as an add-on. For cleaning sticky residue, Everclear grain alcohol works wonders. You could bottle it as a "cleaning solution" or simply recommend it.

Important Sourcing Consideration

When ordering from AliExpress, the product packaging is often dented or damaged during international shipping. It would be a good idea to plan for this. You might consider creating your own branded packaging or using simple, elegant bags to present the heaters to your customers in the best possible way.
