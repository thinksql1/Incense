The Art of Gentle Warming

Welcome to a mindful approach to aromatics. By gently warming, rather than burning, these precious resins and woods, you invite their pure, complex essences into your space without the veil of smoke.

一

The Warming Setup

Classic Incense Heater

The Core Vessel

A specialized heater designed to warm your resins at the perfect temperature, unlocking their true scent profile without scorching them.

View Heater

Incense Trays

Clean Placement

Small trays that hold your resins securely above the heat source, ensuring an even warming process while keeping your main heater pristine.

View Trays

Foil Sheets

No-Mess Practice

Cut these sheets into small squares to line your trays. When the resin is spent, simply dispose of the square. No scraping required.

View Foil

Grill Thermometer

Precision Heat

Different resins melt at different points. This allows you to dial in the exact heat needed, avoiding the harshness of an overheating element.

View Thermometer

Aluminum Boats

Room-filling Scent

Use these directly on the heater as disposable dishes for larger amounts of aromatics when you wish to fill the entire room with fragrance.

View Boats

Japanese Hand Fan

Guiding the Aroma

A traditional method. Use this to gently waft the invisible, aromatic vapors through your space or toward yourself for a mindful listening experience.

二

Aromatics & Woods

Labdanum

Amber & Deep

A thick, tar-like resin. Do not grind. Freeze for 24 hours, break off a pea-sized piece, and place directly on the dish to release rich, sweet, ambery notes.

Procure Resin

Boswellia Sacra

Royal Frankincense

The highest grade of Frankincense. Freeze for 24 hours before grinding. Warmed gently, it sings with bright, uplifting citrus and pine.

Procure Resin

Benzoin Sumatra

Warm Vanilla

Deeply comforting with distinct notes of vanilla. Freeze before breaking down. It pairs beautifully with sharper aromatics, adding a sweet base.

Procure Resin

Greek Monastery

Sandalwood Blend

Crafted in the traditional Athonite method. This fragrant dough is rich with essential oils. Gentle warming ensures the oils release slowly without scorching.

Procure Blend

Myrrh Chunks

Earthy & Grounding

A profoundly grounding, slightly balsamic resin. An ancient partner to Frankincense, it brings depth to meditation. Freeze before breaking into smaller pieces.

Procure Resin

Copal Oro

Golden & Uplifting

Known as 'Golden Copal,' this vibrant resin from Mexico melts cleanly, releasing a crisp, citrus-forward aroma perfect for refreshing a room's energy.

Procure Resin

Sandalwood Chips

Mysore Quality

Pure wood chips. Rather than burning them to ash, gentle heating coaxes out the wood's smooth, creamy, and deeply calming essential oils.
