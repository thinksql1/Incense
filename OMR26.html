A Recap of The Incense Journey™ Sessions

View Retreat Gallery

At the Old Man River Nature Retreat, The Incense Journey became a living bridge between ancient aromatic practice and the deep, grounding rhythm of nature. Removed from the rush of daily life, fragrance was not treated as decoration. It became a doorway: a sensory induction, a silent suggestion, and a way for the body to remember stillness in harmony with the environment.

Guided by John Launius, participants experienced two distinct, multi-layered sessions. Below is the aromatic record of those journeys—a progression of woods, resins, and ancient blends designed to clear the mind, deepen awareness, and integrate the experience into body and memory.

Saturday Session 1

01 Borneol Camphor

A crystalline breath of mountain clarity, opening the senses like cool air moving through an ancient cedar temple.

02 Sandalwood

Soft golden wood unfolding in quiet layers, grounding the mind in stillness and devotional warmth.

03 Frankincense

A luminous resin rising like prayer smoke, carrying the awareness upward into spacious contemplation.

04 Zuikun Jirushi

A refined aloeswood expression where elegance, silence, and hidden sweetness reveal themselves slowly through attentive listening.

05 Oppoponax Sweet Myrrh

Dark honeyed resin with a bittersweet depth, evoking ancient sanctuaries and forgotten rites.

06 Palo Santo

Bright sacred wood with citrus warmth and gentle smoke, cleansing the atmosphere while inviting presence.

07 Labdanum

Rich amber shadows and animalic warmth, wrapping the senses in velvet-like depth and mystery.

08 Kyana-kō

An alchemical nerikō blend of woods, resins, and spices, unfolding like a shifting dream across the incense tray.

09 Dark Amber

Deep resinous warmth with smoky sweetness, like embers glowing beneath twilight silence.

10 Kyphi

The sacred perfume of temple night, weaving wine-dark resins, herbs, and sweetness into a meditative trance.

Saturday Session 2

01 Borneol Camphor

A cooling crystalline gateway that sharpens awareness and clears the inner landscape.

02 Sai-un Nerikō

“Colored Clouds” drifting through the senses in delicate waves of spice, wood, and softened sweetness.

03 Nicotiana Rustica
& Sandalwood

Earthy ceremonial tobacco resting within the calm embrace of sacred sandalwood.

04 Bodai-Shōkō

A contemplative blend inspired by the Bodhi path, balancing serenity, warmth, and subtle spiritual gravity.

05 Frankincense &
Spikenard

Sacred resin and ancient root entwined together in a fragrance of devotion, humility, and anointing.

06 Labdanum

A dense amber resin radiating primal warmth, depth, and quiet sensuality.

07 Kyphi

A timeless temple accord inviting the mind into reverie through layers of resin, herb, and sacred sweetness.
