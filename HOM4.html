On May 17th, we invite you to the HOM Healing Center for a sensory exploration designed to still the mind and open the inner sanctuary. This experience is a curated path through the world's most sacred resins and woods, selected to settle the nervous system and invite a state of steady awareness.

Guided by John Launius, this journey uses fragrance as a bridge to focus. Each material is chosen not just for its aroma, but for its historical and biological ability to clear the gate of the mind, revealing the quiet place that was always there.

Borneol Camphor

Dryobalanops aromatica • Borneo & Southeast Asia

A clearing force. Sharp, penetrating, and immediate. It awakens the senses, cuts through mental fog, and establishes presence.

“A clearing force that establishes presence.”

2

Storax Liquid Amber

Liquidambar formosana • Vietnam

A deep and balsamic resin with amber warmth, soft leather, gentle sweetness, and hints of forest earth. When gently warmed, storax unfolds slowly, creating a rich atmospheric presence that feels ancient, grounding, and enveloping.

“A rich atmospheric presence that feels ancient, grounding, and enveloping.”

Dragon’s Blood

Daemonorops draco • Southeast Asia

A vivid red resin with a mysterious character that bridges brightness and depth. Dragon’s blood carries resinous warmth, subtle fruit-like tones, dry earth, and smoky mineral facets that have made it prized in ritual and sacred spaces for centuries.

“A mysterious character that bridges brightness and depth.”
3
4

Piñon Pine Resin

Trementina • American Southwest

Bright, alive, and expansive, piñon resin evokes sunlit forests and warm desert air. Notes of pine needle, sap, dry wood, and sweet smoke create a feeling of openness, clarity, and natural vitality.

“Evokes sunlit forests and warm desert air.”

Jawee Resin

Benzoin, Dammar, Copal, Storax Blend • Singapore

A beautifully layered resin blend combining sweetness, radiance, and depth. Benzoin offers soft vanilla warmth, dammar contributes luminous clarity, copal brings uplifting brightness, and storax grounds the blend with smooth amber richness.

“A beautifully layered resin blend combining sweetness, radiance, and depth.”
5
6

Sandalwood

Santalum album • India

Soft, creamy, and meditative, true sandalwood carries a refined warmth unlike any other fragrant wood. Its velvety texture and quiet persistence create a calming atmosphere associated with stillness, devotion, and contemplative practice.

“A calming atmosphere associated with stillness, devotion, and contemplative practice.”

Myrrh

Commiphora myrrha • Northeast Africa & Arabia

Bitter, sacred, and deeply contemplative, myrrh reveals layers of dry earth, ancient wood, warm spice, and mineral richness. Its solemn beauty has connected it to spiritual and ceremonial use across countless traditions.

“Bitter, sacred, and deeply contemplative.”
7
8

Palo Santo

Bursera graveolens • South America

Gentle citrus, cool mint, warm wood, and subtle sweetness weave together in this beloved sacred wood. Palo Santo creates a clean and uplifting atmosphere that feels both comforting and expansive.

“A clean and uplifting atmosphere that feels both comforting and expansive.”

Guggul

Commiphora mukul • India

Dark, earthy, and richly textured, guggul carries smoky resin depth with hints of spice and warm leather. Long used in Ayurvedic and devotional traditions, it creates a grounding and immersive aromatic experience.

“Dark, earthy, and richly textured.”
